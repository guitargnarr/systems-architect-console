\section{Executive Summary}
%==============================================================================

This analysis identifies \stat{five critical digital gaps} in Ibanista's current web presence that represent significant competitive disadvantages in the 2026 expat relocation market. Through comprehensive competitor analysis and market research, we validate each gap as a genuine opportunity with quantifiable ROI potential.

\textbf{Key Findings:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item \delivered{Gap Addressed:} Interactive cost calculator---\textit{now live with budget estimation, savings projections, and break-even analysis}
    \item \delivered{Gap Addressed:} Personalized region recommendation tool---\textit{5-question lifestyle quiz with top 3 region matching}
    \item \gap{Gap Confirmed:} No conversational AI assistant---Expat AI and ChatGPT integrations are transforming competitor offerings
    \item \delivered{Gap Addressed:} Email capture with full backend---\textit{FastAPI backend with lead database, email templates, and admin dashboard}

\subsection{Current Digital Presence}

Ibanista's website presents a clean, professional aesthetic with clear service descriptions. However, the site functions primarily as an informational brochure rather than an interactive lead generation platform.

\textbf{Strengths:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Professional design and branding
    \item Clear articulation of UK-France specialization
    \item Strong banking/financial services integration (Currency Cloud)
    \item Bilingual capability (English/French)
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Weaknesses:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item Static content with no interactive tools
    \item No self-service qualification pathway
    \item Manual lead capture only (contact forms)
    \item No personalization or recommendation engine
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Gap 1: No Cost Calculator}

\textbf{Market Evidence:}

UK-to-France moving costs range from \textsterling900 (1-bedroom) to \textsterling2,000+ (full household). Cost of living in France is \stat{31.9\% lower for rent}, \stat{36\% cheaper for transport}, and \stat{39.6\% lower for utilities} compared to UK (2026 data).

\textbf{Features Delivered:}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
    \item \delivered{$\checkmark$} Moving cost estimator (volume-based: studio/partial/full)
    \item \delivered{$\checkmark$} Cost of living comparison (6 French regions)
    \item \delivered{$\checkmark$} Currency display (GBP moving costs, EUR monthly budget)
    \item \delivered{$\checkmark$} Monthly budget projection by household size
    \item \delivered{$\checkmark$} Savings potential calculator (monthly + annual)
    \item \delivered{$\checkmark$} Break-even timeline calculation
\end{itemize}
